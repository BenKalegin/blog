Dancing Stick Figures - for Winamp and Windows Media Player
---------------------------------------------------------------
by Lucian Wischik, based on an original by Justin Frenkel.
http://www.wischik.com/lu/senses/sticky/


This plugin displays a dancing stick figure, dancing in time
to the music. Several stick figures are available. It's
easy to draw your own stick figures as well. Visit
the sticky web site (above) for more information on creating
stick figures, and to download extra ones contributed by
other users.
